Physics Wallah Private Limited (also known as just Physics Wallah or PW) is an Indian educational technology company headquartered in Noida, Uttar Pradesh.[1][2] It was founded by Alakh Pandey and Prateek Maheshwari in 2020. It became a unicorn in June 2022 after raising $100 million at a valuation of $1.1 billion.[3] In June 2022, Physics Wallah became India's 101st unicorn company by raising $100 million in its maiden funding round led by WestBridge Capital and GSV Ventures.[6] During the same time, PW launched its first offline center named Vidyapeeth in Kota, Rajasthan. As of January 2023, Physics Wallah runs over 45 offline centers across 60 cities such as Delhi, Bhopal, Varanasi, Noida, Lucknow, Pune, Kolkata, Siliguri, Guwahati, Malda, Patna, Faridabad ,Ranchi and Srinagar[7]
